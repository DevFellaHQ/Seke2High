Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a campus tour by calling our administration office at +263 123 456 789 or by filling out the contact form on this page. We offer tours on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

What are the school office hours?

Our administrative office is open Monday to Friday from 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM, and on Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM. The office is closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Who should I contact for financial assistance?

For inquiries about fees, payment plans, or financial assistance, please contact our finance office at +263 123 456 789 or email seke2high@gmail.com. We offer flexible payment options to accommodate family budgets.

How do I report a student absence?

To report a student absence, please call the school office at +263 123 456 789 before 8:30 AM on the day of absence. You can also send an email to attendance @seke2high@gmail.com with the student's name, form, and reason for absence.

Where can I find academic calendar information?

The academic calendar is available on our website under the Resources section. You can also request a printed copy from the administration office. The calendar includes term dates, examination periods, and school holidays.
