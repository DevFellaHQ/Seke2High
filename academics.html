Academic Programs

Comprehensive education from Form 1 to Form 6

Form 1 & 2 Curriculum

Our junior secondary program builds a strong foundation in core subjects while introducing students to a wide range of disciplines.

  • English Language & Literature
  • Shona
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Social Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Physical Education
  • Introduction to Practical Subjects

Focus: Building foundational knowledge and skills

Learning Approach

We emphasize interactive learning, critical thinking development, and character building during these formative years.

  • Interactive classroom activities
  • Project-based learning
  • Digital literacy integration
  • Study skills development
  • Extracurricular exploration

Assessment: Continuous assessment with termly examinations

O-Level Program (Forms 3-4)

Students prepare for ZIMSEC Ordinary Level examinations with a balanced curriculum of core and elective subjects.

  • English Language
  • Shona
  • Mathematics
  • Combined Science
  • Geography
  • History
  • Accounts/Business Studies
  • Practical Subject (Choice of one)

Focus: Preparation for O-Level examinations

Subject Combinations

Students can choose from various subject combinations based on their interests and career aspirations.

  • Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Commercial: Accounts, Business Studies, Economics
  • Arts: Heritage, Literature, Art
  • Practical: MTD, WTD, TTD, Agriculture

Assessment: Continuous assessment and mock examinations

A-Level Program (Forms 5-6)

Advanced Level education with specialized subject combinations preparing students for university and professional careers.

  • Sciences: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Commercial: Accounts, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics
  • Arts: History, Literature, Geography, Shona
  • General Studies (Compulsory for all)

Focus: Specialization and university preparation

University Preparation

We provide comprehensive support for students transitioning to higher education.

  • University application guidance
  • Career counseling
  • Research skills development
  • Leadership training
  • Community service projects

Assessment: Continuous assessment and ZIMSEC A-Level examinations
