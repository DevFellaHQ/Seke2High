Admission Requirements

What you need to apply to Seke 2 High School

Form 1 Entry

  • Completed Primary Education (Grade 7)
  • Grade 7 Results Slip or Certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Letter (if applicable)

Form 2-4 Entry

  • Previous School Reports
  • O-Level Results (for Form 5)
  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Letter from Previous School

A-Level Entry (Form 5-6)

  • ZIMSEC O-Level Certificate
  • Minimum 5 O-Level passes including English and Mathematics
  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Letter from Previous School

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common admission questions

    When is the application deadline?

    We accept applications throughout the year, but we recommend applying at least one month before the start of each term to secure your place.

    What documents do I need to submit?

    You'll need a birth certificate, previous school reports, transfer letter (if applicable), and medical report if student perhaps has any medical conditions.(optional) Specific requirements vary by form level.

    Are scholarships available?

    Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students. Contact our admissions office for more information about scholarship opportunities.

    Can I visit the school before applying?

    Absolutely! We encourage prospective students and parents to visit our campus. Please contact us to schedule a tour.

    What is the class size?

    We maintain small class sizes with an average of 40 - 45 students per class to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.
