About Seke 2 High School

Discover our rich history, mission, and the dedicated team that makes Seke 2 High School a premier educational institution

Our Story

Established in 1985, Seke 2 High School has been at the forefront of providing quality education in Chitungwiza and beyond. For nearly four decades, we have nurtured young minds, shaping them into responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our school takes pride in its consistent high pass rates and academic excellence. We've built a reputation as one of the top government schools in the region, offering a comprehensive curriculum that balances academics, practical skills, sports, and extracurricular activities.

At Seke 2 High, we believe in holistic education that develops not just the mind but also character, creativity, and critical thinking skills. Our zero discrimination policy ensures that every student receives equal opportunities to thrive and succeed.

Our History

Milestones in our journey of educational excellence

1985

Foundation

Seke 2 High School was established with the vision of providing quality secondary education to the growing community of Chitungwiza.

1992

First A-Level Class

We expanded our curriculum to include Advanced Level education, offering students the opportunity to continue their studies beyond O-Level.

2001

Science Laboratory Upgrade

State-of-the-art science laboratories were established, enhancing our practical science education capabilities.

2010

Computer Lab Installation

We introduced a modern computer laboratory, bringing digital literacy and technology education to our students.

2018

Smart Classroom Initiative

Interactive smart boards were installed in classrooms, revolutionizing teaching and learning methodologies.

2023

Solar Power Installation

We implemented a solar power backup system, ensuring uninterrupted learning even during power outages.
